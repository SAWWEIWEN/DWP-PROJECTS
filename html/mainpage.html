Introduction

Welcome to Forest Ridge Resort, where serenity meets the embrace of nature. Our resort, nestled in a beautiful, expansive forest,

provides a peaceful respite from the rush and bustle of everyday life. Immerse yourself in nature's beauty as you walk through

the wonderful wooded trails, inhaling in the crisp, clean air. The majestic canopy of towering trees above casts dappled sunshine on

the forest floor, where a diverse tapestry of plant and fauna awaits your discovery.

Our rooms fit nicely with the natural environment, giving a harmonious blend of comfort and rustic appeal. Each accommodation has a balcony

with a view of the forest, encouraging you to wake up to the sight of sun-kissed treetops and fall asleep to the calming sounds of nocturnal nature.

For the adventurous at heart, Forest Ridge Resort has lots of outdoor activities that will capture your interest. Explore hidden waterfalls and secret

groves on a guided walk, or test your abilities on our difficult mountain bike tracks. Immerse yourself in the tranquillity of the forest with a yoga class

in our open-air pavilion, which is surrounded by towering trees and the delicate whisper of leaves.
